Our concept

Students exchanging clothes they are not wearing anymore

Start Now

1. How does it work?

We connect students who are willing to renew their wardrobe at lower cost. Indeed, girls and boys from the same city or not, can exchange their pieces of clothing they no longer wear. It is both a saving for your student budget and a saving for the planet because you do not have to buy anything new.

2. Who are we?

This is Marie Mariotte the founder of the 'My Fabrics' platform. With five of her friends she managed to put this idea and her experience as a student into a business
